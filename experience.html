My Experience while study and intern

  • Life has been full of experiences that have shaped me into the person I am today. While studying for my diploma in Information Management at UiTM, I’ve had the chance to grow academically and personally. One of the most significant milestones was completing my internship, which gave me a glimpse into the professional world and how things work beyond the classroom. It wasn’t just about putting what I’d learned into practice; it was about learning how to adapt, communicate effectively, and contribute to something bigger.

  • The internship taught me to stay organized, manage tasks efficiently, and work with others to achieve goals. I also learned how to handle challenges, especially when things didn’t go as planned, which helped me become more resourceful and flexible. Each day was different, and I felt proud knowing that my contributions made a difference, even in small ways.

  • Outside of my studies and internship, personal projects and challenges have also played a big role in my growth. Learning programming languages like C++ and MySQL taught me patience and persistence, while designing my website allowed me to explore my creativity and develop new skills. These experiences, along with the lessons from my internship, have boosted my confidence and prepared me to embrace new challenges as I continue to learn and grow.
